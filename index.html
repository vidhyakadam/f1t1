The Beagle

The Beagle is a breed of small hound, similar in appearance to the Foxhound. It is a scent hound, developed primarily for hunting hare.

Trivia

  1. The Beagle breed is known for its exceptional sense of smell.
  2. Beagles are often used as detection dogs at airports due to their keen sense of smell.
  3. The breed is friendly, curious, and great with families.
  4. Beagles come in a variety of coat colors, including tricolor, lemon, and red and white.
